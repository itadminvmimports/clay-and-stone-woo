Straight from the medina to your home

Every piece in our collection has been hand-selected during visits to Morocco — chosen not just for beauty, but for the story it carries and the hands that made it.

How we source

We travel directly to the potters of Marrakech, Fes, Safi, and the Atlas Mountain villages. We buy from families who have been working clay for generations — not from wholesalers or middlemen.

This means every piece is genuinely handcrafted, genuinely Moroccan, and genuinely unique. No two are exactly alike.

What we bring back

Our focus is pots and vases — from dramatic statement pieces like our Picasso-style painted vases to humble terracotta garden urns that have been made the same way for centuries. We look for pieces with character, with texture, with a life already lived in them.

Our San Diego showroom

We're part of the Villa & Mission Imports family in San Diego — Southern California's premier source for Mediterranean and Latin architectural finishes. Come see the pieces in person. Touch them. Understand their scale. Our team can help you find exactly the right piece for your space.
